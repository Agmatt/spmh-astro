St. Paul's Mission Hospital will be hosting a free Community Health Camp on Saturday 12th April 2026, open to all residents of Homa Bay County and surrounding areas.

## Services Available

The following services will be available free of charge on the day:

- General medical consultations
- HIV testing and counselling
- Maternal and child health screenings
- Blood pressure and diabetes screening
- Pharmacy — free medicines for conditions identified on the day

## Who Should Come

This camp is open to everyone — no referral, no appointment, no cost. We particularly encourage community members who do not regularly access healthcare to attend.

## Getting There

The camp will be held on the St. Paul's Mission Hospital grounds, off Governor Cyprian Awiti Street, Homa Bay Town. Follow the signage from the main gate.
